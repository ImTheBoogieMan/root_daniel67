Capython Project: Capyhoot

Team Members: Alina, Daniel, Cameron, Fiona, Flavia, Felipe, Kiara

Description: Our prompt was "Educate" and "Achieving engaging and inclusive student learning". So, our team decided to do a bootleg version of Kahoot called "Capyhoot". We made it so that the questions in the Capyhoot are themed around Capyskills.

Role: My role in the team was to help with some of the logo stuff and creating 20 custom graphics for our game, since adding our own custom graphics would be nice.

Challenges: One of the challenges that we had as a group is the amount of time allocated for the project. We were able to manage through it, but we had to LOCK IN to get pass through that challenge.
